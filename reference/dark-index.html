The distinction

Pumphrey & Co. is a machine shop first.

That distinction matters.

Most restoration shops can clean and paint a car. Very few can machine a period-correct engine, pour Babbitt bearings from scratch, or weld cast iron without compromising the metal.

Matt Pumphrey built this shop around those capabilities. Restoration is what that capability produces.
